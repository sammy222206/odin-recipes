Deep Dish BBQ Chicken Pizza:

Deep Dish BBQ Chicken Pizza is every pizza lovers dream! BBQ sauce is spread onto fresh pizza dough, topped with red onions, chicken, a blend of cheeses, and freshly chopped cilantro, to make the most delicious pizza.

Deep dish BBQ chicken pizza is a mouthwatering pizza variant that combines the rich flavors of barbecue sauce, tender chicken, gooey cheese, and a thick, buttery crust. It's a popular American-style pizza that offers a delightful blend of savory and tangy flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Flavor
  • Chicken/Beef
  • Gooey cheese
  • Butter
  • vegetables
  • Barbecue Sauce
  • Seasonings

Steps for making this dish:

  1. Prepare the dough: If making the pizza dough from scratch, follow a recipe or use pre-made dough. Let the dough rise and come to room temperature according to the instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven: Preheat the oven to the recommended temperature, typically around 425°F (220°C), or as specified in your recipe.
  3. Prepare the pizza pan: Grease a deep-dish pizza pan with oil or cooking spray to prevent sticking. Make sure the pan is well coated, including the sides.
  4. Shape the dough: Take the pizza dough and press or stretch it to cover the bottom and sides of the greased pizza pan. Ensure an even thickness throughout. Let it rest for a few minutes to relax the gluten.
  5. Spread the barbecue sauce: Take a generous amount of barbecue sauce and spread it evenly over the dough, covering the entire surface.
  6. Add the chicken/beef and toppings: Place the cooked and sliced or shredded chicken evenly over the barbecue sauce layer. Add any additional toppings like sliced onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, or pineapple according to your preference.
  7. Cheese it up: Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheese over the toppings, covering them entirely. Use a combination of cheeses like mozzarella, cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a cheese blend for added flavor and texture.
  8. Bake the pizza: Place the pizza pan in the preheated oven and bake for the recommended time, usually around 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Garnish and serve: Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly. Sprinkle fresh chopped cilantro or parsley on top for a burst of freshness. Allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
