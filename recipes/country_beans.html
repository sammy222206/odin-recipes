Country Style Baked Beans:

Country Style Baked Beans are sweet, saucy, and very delicious with both beef and fish. We’ve got you covered with all the steps required to prepare this recipe.

Country style baked beans are a popular dish typically associated with American cuisine. They are a hearty and flavorful combination of beans, typically white beans or navy beans, slow-cooked in a savory sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Big black beans
  • Mustard
  • Palm oil
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Beef
  • Fish
  • Cmoky flavor

Steps for making this dish:

  1. Soak the beans: Start by soaking the beans overnight in water.
  2. Prepare the ingredients: Gather all the necessary ingredients, including the beans, and sauce ingredients.
  3. Cook the beans: In a large pot or Dutch oven, add the soaked beans and cover them with water.
  4. Prepare the sauce: In a separate bowl, mix together the sauce ingredients, adjusting the quantities to suit your taste preferences.
  5. Combine beans and sauce: Add the drained beans to a baking dish or casserole dish. Pour the sauce over the beans and stir gently to coat them evenly.
  6. Bake the beans: Cover the dish with a lid or aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at around 350°F (175°C) for approximately 1 to 1.5 hours.
  7. Optional: Add toppings: If desired, you can add additional toppings during the last 15 minutes of baking.
  8. Serve and enjoy: Once the beans are cooked and the sauce has thickened, remove from the oven and let them cool slightly. Serve the country style baked beans as a delicious side dish to complement your meal.
